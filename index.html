How to Earn DAP (Contribute-to-Earn)

Audit the Auditors

Stake DAP to review our data sets. Earn DAP for finding bugs or inaccuracies, boosting trust and aligning incentives.

B2B Onboarding Bounties

Stake or burn DAP to nominate projects for audits. If they become a client, you get a referral bonus in DAP.

Data Mining Bounties

We post bounties for specific data needs. Stake DAP to participate, and winners receive DAP rewards.

How to Use DAP (Platform Utility)

Access Premium Data

Stake DAP for priority access to signals, lower latency, and exclusive "Alpha Drop" reports.

Govern the Ecosystem

Use your DAP to vote on which new data feeds, integrations, or platform features to launch next.

Sponsor New Assets

Burn DAP to sponsor new tokens or equities for coverage. Be a "Signal Pioneer" and earn a share of the data revenue.
